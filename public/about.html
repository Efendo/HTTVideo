About

The idea of HTTVideo originated from one of my other sites, MovingBricks.
The problem with MovingBricks was, i had to change the source code to upload a video.
I spent 2 days developing the basic site and systems.
First it was called "VidPlatform" during development, then it was called "PlayMotion".
Then i found out theres a company called PlayMotion, so i changed it to "HTTVideo".
